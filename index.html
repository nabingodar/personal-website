About

A student of places, and of thinking itself.

I'm Nabin Godar, originally from Pokhara, Nepal. My path has taken me from the hills of Nepal to Japan, and now toward the United Kingdom to study International Business.

I care about international trade and export promotion, especially opportunities for Nepal's economy, and I'm endlessly curious about technology, philosophy, and the world's untold stories.

This site is where I keep both halves of that: the record of where I've been, and the ideas I'm still working through.
