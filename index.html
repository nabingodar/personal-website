About

A student of places, and of thinking itself.

I'm Nabin Godar, originally from Pokhara, Nepal. My path has taken me from the hills of Nepal to Japan, and now toward the United Kingdom to study International Business Management — but the real journey has always been about how different places teach you to think differently.

I care about international trade and export promotion, especially opportunities for Nepal's economy, and I'm endlessly curious about technology, philosophy, and evidence-based reasoning — the kind of thinking that holds up under scrutiny, wherever it takes you.

This site is where I keep both halves of that: the record of where I've been, and the ideas I'm still working through.
